Why Feowl?

Africa is literally the Dark Continent , in terms of citizen's access to and consumption of civil energy
-Earth at night published by Nasa on its website

In 2009, Electrificatioin rate in Sub Saharan Africa was:
30.5% in Urban areas
14.2% in Rural areas
Even you with access suffer from frequent and untimely power cuts

2 surveys by the World bank:
Survey 1: 30 power cuts a year
Survey 2: 120 power cuts a year

Survey1: bit.ly/yu5ybv. Survey2:bit.ly/zctdG5

The PROBLEM:
No data exists to show the real extent of the problem
No data exists that could hold power utilities to account

How do we understand, quantify and geo-localise the information on the energy demand in emerging large cities of Sub-Saharan Africa ?

FEOWL(read fuel) believes what can be measured can be changed

What is Feowl

Energizing fuel + watchful owl

Feowl is a crowd-sourcing open data platform that produces reliable and actionable public data on the lack of electricity

How it works?

  1. Feowl is a bottom-up data collector that reflects the situation of every electricity user in Douala.
  2. Feowl mixes crowdsourcing and polling methods to produce the most accurate and relevant data.
  3. Feowl questions a panel of contributors regularly

3 QUESTIONS:

  1. How many power cuts did you have today?
  2. How long did they last?
  3. In which neighborhood were you?
The answers are automatically analyzed and displayed on the web platform, www.feowl.com/explore

FEOWL BETA and results

100 Users where polled through: SMS, www.feowl.com and via Email over 6months.
The results are found on www.feowl.com

Result

The collected data can report with Some certainty that on a given day, in a given neighborhood, X ,10 people experienced at least one power cut and that the average duration of the cut was Y minutes.
